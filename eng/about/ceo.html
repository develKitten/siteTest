CEO Message

Greetings,

Our company was established to enhance global competitiveness by localizing advanced equipment and precision control systems essential for semiconductor manufacturing processes. We provide a wide range of equipment, including temperature control systems, ultra-precision flow control valves, high-temperature flow meters, wafer transfer robots, and RF plasma solutions, to meet the sophisticated demands of the semiconductor industry.

Moving forward, we will continue to support innovation in semiconductor processes through relentless research and development, delivering the highest quality products and services to our valued customers. We place the utmost importance on our customers’ success and trust, and we consider continuous investment in technological development and securing advanced technologies as our core values.

We are committed to becoming a company that tirelessly provides tailored core solutions and consulting to foster mutual growth and success with our customers.
