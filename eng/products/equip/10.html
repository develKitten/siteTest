Equipment Front End Modulem

Key Features


• A standard interface module for process equipment that supplies wafers to process modules in semiconductor lines.


Components


• Load Port: FOUP (Front Opening Universal Pod)

• Carrier Door Opener: Automatically opens the FOUP

• Mini Environment: Equipped with a filter module and ionizer to minimize particle contamination

• Door Interlock: A safety device that stops the equipment when the door is opened
